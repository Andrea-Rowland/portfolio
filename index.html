About Me

For the past 14 years, I have been working in Higher Education. I've helped students transition from high school to college succesfully, ensured they made the most of their first year at the University, and assisted them in finding research positions and presenting/publishing their work. My experience at the University has given me the opportunity to work with people in various demographics, learn how to operate successfully in a large University, and manage programs and people to produce optimal results.


Ready for a new challenge, I decided to enroll in the University of Utah's Full Stack Development Bootcamp. I'm thrilled to be learning new concepts and languages and finding ways to implement them in my work.
